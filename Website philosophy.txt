Review the website please: Charaivati.com

There are 6 levels - Self, Society, State, Nation, Earth, Universe.
Each layer has different sections for effective analysis and work.

Navigation:

Click the link Charaivati.com or .in or .org

Select your language
Log in/ register or continue as guest

First you come to Self layer:

First section/Tab is Personal:

Select one or two driving force of your life.
4 options are given based on 4 varnas of Hinduism.

Then inside the driving force, write your goals.
You can generate milestones for the goals - AI assisted.

You need Skills, Health, Network and Money to achieve
the goals - Set these parameters to analyse and make
action plan.

After setting all these parameters, you should come to action 
mode. This will hide all the above, only showing your driving
force and give you the todo list of daily, weekly monthly.
You can set your interval (like 3 days repeating tasks).

Second Tab is Social
Your networking activities, chats, posts are here.

Third tab is Learning tab:
Here you improve your skills that you set in Personal tab.
You keep a tutor, watch videos from other creators related
to your skills.

Fourth layer is Earning Tab:
Here you create business pages. Do idea evaluation. Setup
stores to sell your skill. This ends the Self layer.
