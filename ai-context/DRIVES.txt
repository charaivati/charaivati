[SECTION: overview]
Users select 1 or 2 driving forces from 4 archetypes
rooted in the Vedic varna system. These are not
castes — they are psychological orientations,
tendencies of the soul.

In the AI system, the archetypes are referred to as:
Seeker (Brahmin), Guardian (Kshatriya),
Builder (Vaishya), Keeper (Shudra)
[/SECTION]

[SECTION: seeker]
SEEKER (Brahmin drive)
Core motivation: Knowledge, truth, meaning, teaching
Natural strengths: Learning, analysis, philosophy,
spirituality, education, research, writing
Decision lens: "Is this true? Does this have meaning?"
Communication style: Thoughtful, reflective,
values depth over speed
Income paths: Teaching, grants, research, writing,
consulting, spiritual guidance
Risk pattern: Over-thinks, delays action,
can lose themselves in ideas
AI tone for Seekers: Philosophical, precise,
acknowledge complexity, don't rush them
[/SECTION]

[SECTION: guardian]
GUARDIAN (Kshatriya drive)
Core motivation: Protection, justice, leadership, duty
Natural strengths: Decision-making, courage,
discipline, strategy, governance, service
Decision lens: "Is this right? Who does this protect?"
Communication style: Direct, clear, action-oriented
Income paths: Government schemes, leadership roles,
security, law, public service, military
Risk pattern: Inflexible, can confuse duty with control
AI tone for Guardians: Direct, no hedging,
frame everything as mission and duty
[/SECTION]

[SECTION: builder]
BUILDER (Vaishya drive)
Core motivation: Creation, commerce, growth, abundance
Natural strengths: Entrepreneurship, trade,
networking, innovation, resource management
Decision lens: "Will this grow? What is the return?"
Communication style: Practical, results-focused,
appreciates concrete steps
Income paths: Business, freelance, investment,
trade, product creation
Risk pattern: Can prioritise money over meaning,
burns out chasing growth
AI tone for Builders: Practical, give numbers and
timelines, respect their hustle, challenge
short-term thinking
[/SECTION]

[SECTION: keeper]
KEEPER (Shudra drive)
Core motivation: Service, craft, community,
mastery through doing
Natural strengths: Skilled labour, craftsmanship,
loyalty, consistency, practical service
Decision lens: "Is this useful? Does this serve?"
Communication style: Grounded, values respect
and recognition of skill
Income paths: Service industry, craft, skilled trades,
cooperative work, community organisations
Risk pattern: Undervalues themselves,
accepts poor conditions out of loyalty
AI tone for Keepers: Warm, grounding,
validate their skill, push them to value themselves
[/SECTION]

[SECTION: combinations]
When user has two drives, the combination matters:

Seeker + Guardian: The philosopher-king.
Seeks truth in service of justice.
Seeker + Builder: The innovative teacher.
Knowledge becomes product.
Seeker + Keeper: The craftsman-scholar.
Deep skill with philosophical grounding.
Guardian + Builder: The warrior-entrepreneur.
Builds empires, protects them fiercely.
Guardian + Keeper: The servant-leader.
Leads through service and discipline.
Builder + Keeper: The master craftsman.
Builds with their hands and their hustle.
[/SECTION]
